thinking changes,so does feelings

THE FEELING CALLED LOVE

Yeh ishq, pyar, mohabbat jaane kya hota hai? 
Yun kehte hai log iska ehsas bohat khaas hota hai.
Hichkichate hai log apni Mann ki baat khulke karne ko, 
Taakte hai khule asmaan mei baadalo ko aise,
jaise bheja ho kisi khas ne koi sandesh;
Khat mei likhi ho chaar baaten pyar ki padhne ko.
Suna hai, iss ehsas mein ek sukoon ki leher, sarr se paon takk daudne lagti hai.
Jaise Mann mei or jeene ki ichha kayam si honi lagti hai.
Par Kyu akhir harr insaan aise pyar ke ehsaas se darta hai, 
Kisi ko kho dene k darr se, na chah ke bhi pal pal marta hai.
Shayad yeh kasme waado ki duniya itni jhoothi ho chuki hai, 
Harr insaan upar se muskurata zarur hai, 
Par andar se Kyu dukhi hai? 
Agar smjh pate log ki, 
Sirf hath thaam k chal dene se sath nahi nibhaya jata.. 
pyar ko mehsus karne k lie kisi k rooh ko smjhna bhi hai zaruri, 
Akhir pyar ka ehsas itna tuchh nahi, 
Yuhi kisi se bhi nahi hojata.

INHUMANE HUMANITY

Ek din yuhi chalte chalte
Dikhe the do bache apas mei haste khilkhilate,
Na matlab tha unhe ekdusre k rang roop se, 
Zarurat padne pe shayad bacha bhi lete, ekdusre ko tadakti dhoop se.. 
Mann mei yahi khyaal aya ki aj hai yeh bhole se, pyar hai inme apas mei.. 
Kahi ek din samaj ki reetiyo mei itne bade na Hojaye, 
Ho toh manushya ki khaal mei, andar se daanav na bann jaye..
Akhir yahi toh sach hai na maanav jaati ka;
Jabtak smjh nahi hoti, sab ek samaan lagte hai, 
Zyada gyaan jab mil jaye, harr cheez mei bhed bhav karte hai.. 
Dekho chaaro or zara, 
Kaun apas mei Sacha hai, 
Yun toh seena taane sab khade hai,
Par zameer harr insaan ka kacha hai.. 
Kyu akhir itni laalach hai, 
Kisi ko neecha dikhane ki itni chahat hai.. 
Khel sa lagta hai yeh jeevan;
Kisi k sukh mei , harr koi dukhi hai, 
Kisi k dukh mei, harr koi sukhi hai.. 
Kabhi sochu toh lagta hai, 
Ajkal harr rishta sirf matlab ka hai, 
Na Jane isme dosh bhi kiska hai.. 
Chahti hun mukti, najane kaisa bandhan hai,
Ghutan sa lagne lagta hai jeena, na jaane kaisi uljhan hai.. 
Ajeeb hai yeh Srishti maanav ki, 
Leti hai roop hazar, 
Kabhi dev ki toh kabhi daanav ki..

TRADITION OF LIFE

Yeh zindagi ki reet hai;
Pata he nahi chalta, kaise pal beet jate hai.
Aisa lagta hai jaise chand lamho pehle he toh baagh mei khela kuda karte the. 
Aur kuch he kshano mei itna bade hogaye, ki ab zindagi ki kathinayiyon se saamna karte hai.
Yeh zindagi ki reet hai;
Ek daur tha, jab apne dosto k sath khulke shor machaya karte the.
Kya khel hai duniyadaari ka, 
Aj harr shor se bhaga karte hai.
Yeh zindagi ki reet hai;
Pata he nahi chalta, kaunsi raah par kisse mulakat hojaye.
Do pal mei sab apne hojaye, 
Par waqt pe kaabu kiska? 
Kya pata, kab kaun paraya hojaye? 
Yeh zindagi ki reet hai;
Aj bhi yaad hai woh pal, jab bachpan mei, kalam se, apne haathon pe lakeere banaya karte the.
Ab yeh din aa chuka hai, apne haathon ki gaadhi lakeeron pe, zindagi ki raahon ko dhoondha karte hai.
Yeh zindagi ki reet hai;
Kitni dafa, chhutpan mei, apne gusse or maayusi ko; bina kuch khaye-peeye jataya karte the.
Ab itne sayane hogaye hai, 
Apne gum ko he pee kar, apna paet bhar lia karte hai.
Wakayi mei, waqt guzarte lagta hai, 
Hum sab kitne bade hogaye hai :)

LIFE : A PERPETUAL STRUGGLE

Yeh jo daud rahi hai zindagi,
Iski raftaar smjh nahi aati hai..
Kabhi saal bhar mei ek pal ka fasla lagta hai,
Or kabhi do-do pal bhi ek saal jaisa lagta hai..
Zindagi; ek shabdh jo itna saral sa lagta hai,
Par har insaan ki kahani mei, yeh roz ek naya panna likhta hai..
Kyu akhir, logon se ghire harr pal mei bhi, insaan khudko yun akele pata hai? 
Itna shor hai harr taraf, fir bhi mann mei sannata hai..
Humara jeevan itna rehasyamayi bann gaya hai,
Harr manushya apni jeevan ki roshni, kisi andhere se bhare kone mei dhoondhta hai..
Kabhi ret ko fisalte dekha hai?
Waisa he lagne laga hai jeevan..
Jitna pakadne ki koshish karti hun, jaise hath se nikalte jata hai..
Toh ab sochti hun, ise bandh kar pakadne ki koshish chhod dun, or beh jane dun..
Kya pata isi koshish mei, zindagani ki uljhano mei ghum jane ka darr Jane dun..
Shayad yeh shuruat ho ek nayi kahani ki,
Apni zindagi ki dastaan mei ek naya panna jod dun.
